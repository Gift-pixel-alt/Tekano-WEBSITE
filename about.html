About Us

Tekano Eats is a modern online food ordering platform created in 2025. We connect customers with delicious, affordable meals from local kitchens.

Our mission is to make food ordering simple, fast, and accessible for everyone. We aim to support local food businesses while delivering quality meals to customers.
